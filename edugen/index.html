Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about EduGen.

How does EduGen process my PDFs? +

EduGen uses advanced AI and natural language processing to analyze your PDF documents. It extracts text, identifies key concepts, relationships between ideas, and important facts to generate summaries, questions, and learning materials.

Is my data secure with EduGen? +

Yes, we take data security very seriously. All uploaded documents are encrypted both in transit and at rest. You maintain full ownership of your content, and we never share your materials with third parties.

Can I use EduGen for group study sessions? +

Absolutely! With our Pro and Institution plans, you can share your generated study materials with classmates or students. Our platform supports collaborative learning features perfect for study groups.

What types of PDFs work best with EduGen? +

EduGen works best with well-structured educational materials like textbooks, lecture notes, research papers, and study guides. Documents with clear headings, paragraphs, and academic content yield the best results.

Can I export my study materials? +

Yes, you can export flashcards as Anki decks, quizzes as PDFs or CSV files, and summaries in multiple formats. This allows you to study offline or use materials with other apps.
